How to Login to Your CrashPlan Pro Account: A Step-by-Step Guide

Accessing your CrashPlan Pro account involves a secure, browser-based authentication process, whether you initiate it from the web console or the desktop app [web:3][web:7]. It is crucial to use the correct server address for the Professional tier.

Logging in via the Web Console

The web console is your primary administrative hub. Using the correct URL is the most important step.

  • Step 1: Navigate to the Correct CrashPlan Pro URL. Open your web browser and go to `https://console.us2.crashplanpro.com` [web:3][web:11]. Using a different URL, like the one for the standard enterprise service, will result in a login failure [web:12].
  • Step 2: Enter Your Username. On the sign-in page, enter the email address associated with your CrashPlan Pro account and click 'Sign In' [web:11].
  • Step 3: Provide Your Password and 2FA Code. You will be prompted to enter your password. If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, you will also need to provide the time-sensitive code from your authenticator app on the same screen [web:7].
  • Step 4: Access Your Dashboard. After successful verification, you will be logged into the CrashPlan Pro console, where you can manage your backups, users, and devices.

Logging in from the CrashPlan Desktop App

The desktop app streamlines the login process by integrating with your browser.

  • Step 1: Launch the CrashPlan App. Open the CrashPlan application on your computer.
  • Step 2: Enter Your Username in the App. The app will display a login screen. Enter your CrashPlan Pro username (email address) and click 'Continue' [web:3].
  • Step 3: Complete Authentication in the Browser. The app will automatically open your default web browser to the secure login page. Here, you will enter your password and, if applicable, your 2FA code to complete the authentication [web:3][web:7].
  • Step 4: Automatic App Login. Once you successfully sign in through the browser, the desktop app will automatically be logged in and connected to your account [web:3]. This shared session ensures a secure and seamless experience.

Troubleshooting Common CrashPlan Pro Login Issues

Even with a robust system, you might encounter login hurdles. Here’s how to solve the most frequent CrashPlan Pro login problems.

Forgotten Password or 2FA Issues

If you can't remember your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page (`console.us2.crashplanpro.com`) [web:11]. This will send a reset link to your registered email. If you are having trouble with your two-factor authentication, you can click the "Don’t have your code?" link on the login screen to begin the reset process for your 2FA setup [web:13].

"Unable to sign in. Check the server address."

This specific error is almost always caused by trying to log in through the wrong URL. CrashPlan has different servers for its various products. For CrashPlan Professional and Small Business, you must use the `crashplanpro.com` domain. Manually typing `https://console.us2.crashplanpro.com` into your browser should resolve this issue [web:3][web:12].

App Fails to Open a Browser Window

If the desktop app doesn't automatically launch a browser for you to complete the login, don't worry. The app provides a 'Copy' button. Click this to copy the unique sign-in URL to your clipboard. Then, you can manually paste this URL into a web browser to finish the sign-in process. Once authenticated in the browser, the app will connect automatically [web:3].
